---

## 1. The ask (what we want counsel to do)

Run a **knockout search** and give a **go / amber / no-go opinion** on adopting and
registering **Mentible** for our goods/services, in the classes and jurisdictions below.
This is the fast, register-level pass to surface obvious blockers — **not** a full
availability opinion — with a recommendation on whether to proceed to filing.

We could not do the register-level search ourselves: USPTO TESS, EUIPO eSearch/TMview,
and Trademarkia **block automated access**, so the "is there a *registered* confusable
mark in our classes?" question is still open. That is the core of this engagement.

## 2. The mark

- **Mark:** **MENTIBLE** — a **coined/invented word** (no dictionary meaning).
- **Form:** standard word mark (word only; no stylization/logo at this stage).
- **Exact-spelling signal:** our preliminary sweep found **no product, company, or mark
  using the exact spelling "Mentible"** — supports distinctiveness/ownability. Please
  confirm at register level.

## 3. Goods / services and Nice classes

AI-assisted **educational-content authoring software**, and the compiled learning
artifacts it produces (EPUB3 / PDF "books"). **Adults-only** product (no children's
market). Relevant Nice classes:

| Class | Coverage |
|---|---|
| **9** | Downloadable/SaaS educational-authoring software; generated e-book/PDF learning artifacts |
| **41** | Education / instruction / providing educational content |
| **42** | SaaS — the hosted authoring/generation platform |

## 4. Jurisdictions

- **United States — USPTO.**
- **European Union — EUIPO.** (Included because a key near-miss, below, is an EU-based app.)

## 5. Known potential conflicts to assess (from our preliminary sweep)

**Primary concern — "MENTABLE"** (one letter off, phonetically identical):

| Entity | What it is | Field / likely class | Why it matters |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Mentable, PLLC** (`mentablewellness.com`, Charlotte NC) | Psychiatric / mental-health **clinical practice** (TMS, ADHD testing, telehealth) | Medical/psychiatric **services** (≈ class 44) | Phonetically identical, but a **services business in a different field** (clinical care ≠ learning software) — the different-field gap is the crux |
| **Mentable** (app, Cluj-Napoca, RO; Technovation origin) | Therapist-companion **app** | Mental-health **software** (class 9) | Same stem in **class 9**, EU-based; no EUIPO registration surfaced in our sweep — please confirm |

**Secondary context — crowded "Ment-" edtech namespace:** **Mentimeter** (established),
Menty, Mentiway, Mentornity — education/mentoring tools in 41/42/9. **None is identical or
one-letter-off**, but note for likelihood-of-confusion context.

## 6. The core legal question to pressure-test

US (and EU) **likelihood-of-confusion turns on relatedness of goods/services**, not sound
alone. Both known "Mentable" entities sit in **mental-health / clinical care**; we sit in
**educational-content software**. **Is that different-field gap sufficient** to clear
Mentible in classes 9 / 41 / 42 against a phonetically identical "Mentable," in the US and
the EU — including any **registered** marks our automated sweep could not see?

## 7. Deliverable we're looking for

1. **Go / amber / no-go**, broken out **per class (9/41/42) and per jurisdiction (US/EU)**.
2. Any **registered or pending** marks that materially raise risk (esp. a registered
   "Mentable" or other confusable mark in our classes).
3. A **filing recommendation** — proceed to application, proceed with narrowed
   goods/services, or do not adopt.
4. If **no-go**: a short read on our fallback, **SelfSyllabus** (descriptive/weaker, no
   direct hit found), before we re-clear it.

## 9. Fallback
