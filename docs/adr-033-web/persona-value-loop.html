Mentible · how people get value · learn → author → carry

Your own library, turned into something you learn from and write with

Mentible runs one loop: learn a topic scoped to you, author content grounded in your own library, and carry that library — private — to every device. Here's how that plays out for four very different people.

1 · Learn

Scoped to you

Any topic, at your level, built on what you already know — not a generic chatbot answer.

2 · Author

Grounded in your library

Turn what you keep — books, notes, references — into lessons and documents that cite your own sources.

3 · Carry

Private, every device

Your library follows your account — phone, tablet, web — and stays yours. Never shared, never used to profile you.

The same loop, four lives. Each person leans on the parts that matter most to them.
