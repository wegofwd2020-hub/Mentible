Section 02b — patient data (PHI)

Before you add patient data — the honest version

A "Case notes" tab can hold PHI. Here is exactly where it goes on each tier — so you decide, not discover.

Free · on your device

Your library stays on your device. But when you ask, the relevant passage is sent to the AI model to draft the answer — so it leaves the device for that request. De-identify before entering PHI.

Paid · private hosted

Content lives on our servers — encrypted at rest, access-controlled, but not zero-knowledge. A signed BAA is required before entering PHI. No BAA yet → don't enter identifiable patient data.

We'd rather tell you this up front than have you find out in an audit. Source transparency + your review — you stay the decider.
