Sc. 4 — how I actually work

I read your bible first — then I write, true to your canon

Ask me a question or hand me a scene. I don't invent: I find the relevant details in your bible first, then write consistent with them — and show you which notes I drew on. (Engineers call it RAG. You don't have to.)

›YOU: "Draft a scene brief for the 1922 airfield — keep it true to the bible."

›ME (retrieving): I pull the exact details from your world research & character notes.

›ME (writing): A brief consistent with your world, citing your own notes — no continuity breaks.

★ FEATURE UPGRADE

One day you'll want your world not just on your desk, but on your laptop at the café and your phone in the room. That's when you'll upgrade me.

  • One private library, every device — I carry your whole world and its search across your devices, in sync.
  • Write anywhere — your canon and research, wherever the idea strikes.
  • Your IP stays yours — private, encrypted, never shared, never used to train anyone's model.

The honest version: drafting sends your notes to a third-party AI model; the hosted tier isn't zero-knowledge; and prose you don't substantially rewrite may not be copyrightable — check your WGA/studio terms. Upgrading makes me a private hosted library; the free device-only version stays as it is.
