# Getting started with Mentible — sign in with Google

This guide walks a brand-new user through opening Mentible on the web and creating
an account with Google. It takes about a minute.

> **Where to go:** Mentible runs in your browser at
> **https://mambakkam.net/app/mentible**
> Works in Chrome, Edge, Safari, or Firefox on a laptop or phone — no install needed.

---

## What you get before you even sign in

When you first open Mentible, two finished books are already waiting in your
**Library** — *Claude Certified Architect — Foundations* and *Product Sense and AI*.
You can read them right away without an account.

You only need to sign in when you want to **create your own content** (that requires
adding an API key, covered in the next guide) or to keep your account across devices.

---

## Step-by-step: create your account with Google

1. **Open the app.** Go to **https://mambakkam.net/app/mentible** in your browser.

2. **Open Settings.** In the bottom (or side) navigation, tap **Settings**.

3. **Find the Account panel.** At the top of Settings you'll see an **Account**
   card that says *"Sign in to sync across devices."* Tap it (or tap **Sign in**).

4. **Choose Google.** On the sign-in screen, tap the **Continue with Google**
   button.

5. **Pick your Google account.** A Google window opens. Choose the Google account
   you want to use and approve the permission prompt. Google handles the password —
   Mentible never sees it.

6. **You're in.** The window closes and Mentible returns to your **Library**,
   now signed in. Open **Settings → Account** any time to confirm; it shows
   *"Signed in as"* with your email.

That's it — your account is created on first sign-in. There's no separate "register"
step for Google.

---

## Good to know

- **Prefer email instead?** The same sign-in screen has an email + password form.
  Tap **"No account? Create one"** to register. (Google is the quickest path.)
- **Adults only.** Mentible is an adult self-learning product — no children's
  accounts.
- **What's stored.** Only your Google identity (email) and which providers you've
  added a key for. Mentible never stores what you generate on its servers, and
  your API keys stay on your device (see the next guide).
- **Sign out or switch device.** **Settings → Account → Sign out.** The two bundled
  books stay available; your saved API keys live only in the browser you added them
  in, so you'll re-add them if you sign in on a new device.
